Get your variable width bar charts here!

Instructions for use: Upload a csv with the following four columns. Please include headers as your first row. You can name your headers anything you like, e.g. after members of The Beatles.
item name width of bar height of bar item category
string positive number number string (up to 10 categories allowed)
The generator will rank the items by height in ascending order and colour-code them by category.
Please don't break me, I have zero error handling.
