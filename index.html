Instructions for use: Upload a csv or key in comma-separated values manually. Make sure you have the following four columns, and include headers as your first row. You can name your headers anything you like, e.g. after members of The Beatles.
item name width of bar height of bar item category
string positive number number string (up to 10 categories allowed)
Then choose whether and how you want the data sorted (chart visuals are optimised for ascending order). The items will be colour-coded by category (up to 10 categories allowed for now).

Please don't break me, I have zero error handling. :)
